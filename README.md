# The Law Board

A single-file, offline browser game for historically-themed parties.

## To play

Open `index.html` in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). That's it — no server, no install, no internet required. State is saved to `localStorage`, so refreshing the page keeps the session intact.

## How it works

1. **Setup**: Edit, add, or remove laws. Set the interval between votes. Click "Begin the Session."
2. **Idle**: The Law Board fills with enacted laws as the night goes on. A countdown ticks until the next vote.
3. **Vote**: Either click "Convene the Vote" or wait for the timer. Two random laws appear side by side. Take a verbal vote among your guests and click the winning law.
4. **Enacted**: The winning law gets stamped with a wax seal and joins the board permanently. The loser returns to the pool and may appear in future votes.

Settings (gear icon in the footer) let you change the vote interval mid-game, mute the gavel, or reset the whole session.
